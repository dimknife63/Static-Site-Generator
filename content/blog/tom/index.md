# Why Tom Bombadil Was a Mistake

Tom Bombadil, while charming, distracts from the narrative of the Ring. He appears invulnerable and outside the main plot.

<b>Archmage</b> commentary on Tom's role.

<i>An unpopular opinion, I know.</i>

Tom’s character cannot be ignored, but he serves more as a whimsical aside than a critical figure.

> "Old Tom Bombadil is a merry fellow; bright blue his jacket is, and his boots are yellow."
