# Why Glorfindel is More

Glorfindel played a critical role in the First Age, defending the Last Alliance and aiding in the escape of key characters.

**Legolas** mentions Glorfindel’s deeds.

*Skilled warrior* with unmatched courage.

He embodies the valor and nobility of the elves.

> "He rode with the speed of the wind and the strength of mountains."
